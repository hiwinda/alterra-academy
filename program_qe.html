What is Quality Engineer Program ?

Alterra Academy Full-stack Program is Coding Bootcamp which transform you being professional Quality Engineer within 2 month. You will learn about Software Testing, programming languages, analyzing software item dan evaluate to feature to make sure quality of product. And than you will get chance to be part of our reliable tech team as Quality Engineer.

Registration is done by filling out the online registration form. Click the "Register Now" button to start your registration process. Make sure that you register according to the specified schedule.

Online Test.

April 20th - May 25th 2019

You are required to take an online logic and math test.

Interview Test.

May 6th – May 31th, 2019

Only prospective participants who passed the Online Test will be invited to interview. The Interview Test will be conducted at the Alpha Teknologi Indonesia Office located in Jakarta, Malang or Jambi. Running Program.

12 candidates who passed the Interview Test will be able to join in the Alterra Academy program. Make sure you don't have other commitments on that date. Class will be held in Malang.

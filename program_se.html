What is Full-stack Engineer Program?

Alterra Academy Full-stack Program is Coding Bootcamp which transform you being professional Software Engineer within 3 month. You will learn about many programming languages, build back-end apps microservice concept, build client-site apps, and deploy your apps. And than You will get chance to be part of our reliable tech team as Software Engineer.

Registration is done by filling out the online registration form. Click the "Register Now" button to start your registration process. Make sure that you register according to the specified schedule.

Online Test.

April 20th - May 25th 2019

Interview Test.

May 6th – May 31th, 2019
